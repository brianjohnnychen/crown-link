ABOUT CROWN LINK

About

Your link to service and quality. Crown Link Products Corporation (冠霖產品有限公司) is a Kaohsiung-based trading company that sources specialized and made-to-order fasteners from qualified factories across Taiwan and China — managing production, quality, and shipping on behalf of global industrial manufacturers.

OUR STORY

Your sourcing arm in Taiwan and China

Crown Link exists to solve a specific problem: manufacturers need specialized, hard-to-source, and made-to-order fasteners, but qualifying factories overseas and managing production from afar is slow and risky. We do that work for them. Bring us a drawing or a difficult part, and we find the right factory, manage manufacturing, and own the quality outcome.

Built on factory relationships across Gangshan and greater Kaohsiung — and extended into qualified production in China — Crown Link brings decades of combined sourcing experience to every program, with the factory-floor access that remote purchasing cannot replicate.

GANGSHAN, KAOHSIUNG

Where the world's fasteners are made

Taiwan is the world's third-largest fastener exporter. Gangshan District in Kaohsiung is where the industry is concentrated — hundreds of specialized factories within a few kilometers of Crown Link's office.

This proximity enables factory-floor oversight that sourcing from a remote office cannot replicate: tooling checks before production, in-process audits during the run, and face-to-face corrective action when it matters.

WHY MANUFACTURERS WORK WITH US

What you get from a sourcing partner on the ground

Crown Link is trusted to source and supply parts for leading global industrial manufacturers — handling the factory side so our customers can focus on their own products and programs.

  • Specialized parts — We take on the hard-to-source and made-to-order fasteners that are difficult to buy off a catalog. Submit a drawing and Crown Link sources to spec.
  • Factory access — Tooling checks before production, in-process audits during the run, and face-to-face corrective action across our qualified Taiwan and China factory network.
  • Independent quality — 3-tier inspection catches problems at the factory before shipment. We verify quality ourselves rather than relying on the factory to grade its own work.
  • Documentation — An English-speaking, documentation-fluent team prepares complete export paperwork — commercial invoice, packing list, and country-of-origin records — for clean importing.
  • Lead times — Standard 6–10 weeks FOB Kaohsiung, with air freight available for urgent orders and forecast-based scheduling on repeat programs.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

MOQ varies by product type, material, and surface finish. Many standard items have no formal MOQ for initial orders. Contact us with your specification for a specific MOQ and lead time quote.

Do you provide samples?

Yes. Sample availability and cost depend on the product. Standard catalog items can typically be sampled within 2–3 weeks. Custom per-drawing parts require tooling lead time.

Can you source items not shown in your catalog?

Yes. Crown Link's sourcing capability extends well beyond the catalog. Submit a drawing, specification, or description and we will identify qualified manufacturers and provide a quote.

What payment terms do you offer?

Standard terms are T/T (wire transfer): 30% deposit upon order confirmation, 70% balance prior to shipment. Other arrangements available for established accounts.

How do I submit a drawing for a custom part?

Email your drawing in PDF, DWG, DXF, STEP, or IGES format to crown.link@msa.hinet.net with your quantity, material, finish, and required standards. Include your company name and a contact name. We will respond with questions or a quote within 3–5 business days.

What incoterms do you use?

Standard shipments are quoted FOB Kaohsiung Port. CIF to US West Coast ports available upon request. Door-to-door DDP pricing available for established customers.
