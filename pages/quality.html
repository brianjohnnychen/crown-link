QUALITY SYSTEM

Quality

Three independent inspection tiers, Level III PPAP documentation, and NIST-traceable gauging on every order. At Crown Link, the factory never grades its own work.

3-TIER INSPECTION

Three tiers. Three checkpoints. Zero self-grading.

TIER 1

Pre-Production

Equipment qualification inspection at the factory before production begins. We verify tooling condition, raw material certification, machine calibration records, and process parameters against Crown Link specifications.

TIER 2

In-Process

Mid-production sampling and process audit. Dimensional checks on in-process parts, surface finish spot-checks, and thread gauging during the production run. Deviations trigger immediate corrective action before the full run completes.

TIER 3

Independent Post-Production

Third-party inspection by an independent quality agency not affiliated with the factory, with results reported directly to Crown Link. This tier is non-negotiable on all orders. The factory does not control the final quality verdict.

LEVEL III PPAP

Level III PPAP — managed from Taiwan

What PPAP is. The Production Part Approval Process is the automotive industry's standardized method (AIAG) for demonstrating that a supplier's production process can reliably produce parts meeting all engineering requirements — before volume production begins.

Why buyers require it. Automotive and industrial OEM buyers require PPAP because it shifts quality proof from promises to documented evidence: process capability studies, measurement system analysis, and dimensional results on actual production parts, all signed under a Part Submission Warrant.

What Level III means. Level III is the default and most comprehensive standard submission: the complete documentation package plus sample parts is submitted to the customer for approval — not merely retained at the supplier.

How Crown Link manages PPAP from Taiwan. Crown Link coordinates the entire package with the producing factory: design records, process flow diagrams, PFMEA, control plans, MSA, dimensional results, material and performance test results from qualified laboratories, initial process studies, appearance approval, checking aids, customer-specific requirements, sample parts, and the signed PSW.

Your APQP team communicates with one English-speaking point of contact — Crown Link — while we handle factory-floor coordination in Gangshan. See the full package contents on our Capabilities page.

TESTING

Testing Procedures

  • Dimensional Inspection — CMM, optical comparator, digital calipers, height gauges, and go/no-go thread gauges
  • Tensile & Proof Load Testing — per ASTM F606 and ISO 898
  • Prevailing Torque Testing — for locknuts per ISO 2320 and ASME B18.16.6
  • Salt Spray / Corrosion Resistance — per ASTM B117, with results reported in hours to white and red rust
  • Rockwell Hardness Testing — HRB / HRC per ASTM E18
  • Thread Gauging — per ASME B1.1 for UNC/UNF and ASME B1.13M for metric, with inspection gauges calibrated to NIST-traceable standards
  • Coating Thickness Measurement — magnetic induction per ISO 2178 for zinc and nickel on steel, and X-ray fluorescence (XRF) for plating thickness verification
