INDUSTRIES SERVED

Industries

Seven sectors, one sourcing discipline. Crown Link sources specialized and made-to-order fasteners from Taiwan and China for industrial manufacturers across the industries below — each with its own standards, finishes, and documentation requirements.

Automotive-Adjacent & Aftermarket

Vehicle and aftermarket programs call for consistent quality, tight dimensional tolerances, and durable surface finishes such as Dacromet and Geomet for long-cycle corrosion resistance. Documentation and traceability requirements are common, and PPAP-level packages may be requested for production parts.

Crown Link capability: Crown Link provides Level III PPAP documentation capability, Dacromet and Geomet finishing, tight-tolerance sourcing, and 3-tier independent inspection where program requirements call for it.

Industrial Machinery & Equipment

Machinery builders need custom per-drawing parts far more often than catalog items. Material requirements span carbon steel through stainless and alloy grades, frequently on the same bill of materials. Structural bolting, springs, castings, and machined components all converge in a single build.

Crown Link capability: Crown Link consolidates the full BOM — custom fasteners, springs, castings with secondary machining, and structural bolting — under one quote and one quality system.

Electronics & Electrical Enclosures

Electronics assembly runs on small-size precision screws in the M2–M4 range with consistent drive engagement and plating. Non-magnetic stainless grades matter near sensitive components, and SEMS assemblies speed PCB mounting. Stamped panel hardware and hole plugs complete enclosure builds.

Crown Link capability: Crown Link supplies miniature precision screws, 304/316 non-magnetic stainless, SEMS assemblies, stamped panel hardware, and knockout hole plugs from Taiwan's electronics fastener base.

Construction & Infrastructure

Structural connections call for large diameter high-strength bolts in Grade 8, A325, and A490 classes with verified mechanical properties. Hot-dip galvanized finish per ASTM A153 is standard for exposed steelwork. Anchor systems and heavy stampings round out structural hardware packages.

Crown Link capability: Crown Link sources hot-forged structural bolting, HDG finishing per ASTM A153, anchor hardware, and heavy-gauge stampings with full mill certification.

Agricultural Equipment

Farm equipment lives in mud, fertilizer, and washdown chemicals — corrosion-resistant coatings are a service-life requirement. Grade 5 and Grade 8 fasteners carry the structural loads, while custom stampings form equipment brackets. High-cycle springs endure constant vibration and shock.

Crown Link capability: Crown Link supplies Grade 5 and Grade 8 hardware with Dacromet, HDG, and zinc finishes, custom bracket stampings, and load-tested high-cycle springs.

Consumer Products & Appliances

Consumer-facing hardware must look as good as it functions — decorative nickel and chrome finishes are specified alongside mechanical requirements. Assembly kits and poly-bagged packaging to spec keep production lines and aftermarket channels moving. SEMS assemblies speed appliance panel assembly.

Crown Link capability: Crown Link provides decorative finishing, kitted and poly-bagged packaging to your BOM with custom header cards, and SEMS assemblies for panel production.

Aerospace & Defense (Commercial Hardware)

Commercial-grade aerospace and defense hardware demands full material traceability from melt to finished part. Calibration records must be NIST-traceable, tolerances are tight, and PPAP documentation is frequently required even outside automotive contexts. Stainless and alloy steel to exact customer specification is the norm.

Crown Link capability: Crown Link maintains full material traceability, NIST-traceable gauge calibration, PPAP documentation capability, and stainless/alloy sourcing to customer spec for commercial-grade hardware.
