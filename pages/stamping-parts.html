Stamping Parts

Taiwan's stamping industry runs from high-speed progressive dies producing millions of clips per month to 800-ton presses forming structural brackets. Crown Link sources across that range — retaining rings, washers, weld nuts, panel hardware, and per-drawing custom stampings — with tooling cost quoted transparently up front. Material certifications and dimensional reports accompany every production shipment.

Hose Clamp

Worm-drive clamps with perforated 300-series stainless bands per SAE J1508, sized from 8 mm to over 250 mm diameter. The universal sealing clamp for automotive coolant, fuel, industrial hose, and ducting connections.

Tee Nut

Stamped T-nuts with a flanged head, hollow threaded barrel, and pronged anchors that embed into wood and composite panels. Standard hardware for furniture, climbing holds, speaker cabinets, and any wood joint needing a durable machine thread.

Request Quote

Stamped Parts Per Drawing

Custom stampings to your print — brackets, clips, contacts, and chassis parts with progressive-die forming, embossing, and piercing in steel, stainless, brass, and aluminum. Tooling quoted up front with per-piece pricing that drops sharply at volume.

Request Quote

Flat Washer

Plain washers per ASME B18.21.1 and DIN 125 in carbon steel, stainless, brass, and nylon, in standard and fender (wide OD) patterns. They spread bearing load, protect surfaces, and span oversized or slotted holes.

Request Quote

Stamped Weld Nut

Sheet-metal weld nuts stamped with an extruded thread barrel and embossed weld projections — lighter and lower cost than machined weld nuts. Used at high volume in automotive panels and appliance frames where joint loads permit the thinner thread engagement.

Request Quote

C-Clip, E-Clip & Tee Washer

External retaining rings — C-clips and E-clips per DIN 6799 and ASME B18.27 — that seat in shaft grooves to axially locate bearings, gears, and pins, alongside stamped tee washers for channel and slot retention. Supplied in spring steel and stainless with phosphate or zinc finishes.

Request Quote

Push Nut

Stamped spring-steel fasteners whose angled fingers slide over a plain stud or shaft in one direction and bite against removal. A one-push, no-thread retention solution for axles, trim studs, and captive-hardware assemblies.

Request Quote

Hole Plug

Stamped steel and stainless dome plugs that snap into punched knockout holes to seal electrical enclosures, panels, and tubing ends. Sized to standard knockout diameters with zinc, painted, or stainless finishes.

Request Quote

Large Stamping Part

Heavy-gauge structural stampings — channel brackets, mounting plates, and reinforcement members up to thick-plate gauges with multi-stage bending and piercing. Sourced from Taiwan press shops running 200–800 ton lines with in-die sensing for dimensional consistency.
