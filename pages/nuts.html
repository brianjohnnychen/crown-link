Nuts

Crown Link supplies nuts from high-volume cold-forged hex stock through prevailing-torque locknuts per ASME B18.16.6, projection weld nuts, and fully machined custom geometries. Thread acceptance is verified with go/no-go gauging calibrated to NIST-traceable standards on every order. If your nut does not exist in a catalog, send the drawing — custom geometry sourcing is a core Crown Link service.

Conical Nut

Hex nuts with a conical bearing face that self-centers in chamfered or spherical seats, most familiar as wheel lug nut geometry. The taper distributes clamp load evenly and resists loosening in seated joints under vibration.

Request Quote

Flange Nut

Hex nuts with an integral wide washer flange, available with serrations that bite into the mating surface for vibration resistance, per ASME B18.2.2. They eliminate a separate washer, speed assembly, and spread bearing load over soft or painted surfaces.

Request Quote

Nylon Insert Locknut

Prevailing-torque locknuts per DIN 985 / ASME B18.16.6 with an undersized nylon collar that grips the bolt thread elastically. The most widely specified vibration-resistant nut in general industry, reusable for limited cycles and effective without thread adhesives.

Request Quote

Large Pipe Coupling Nut

Elongated hex nuts with straight machine threads for joining two lengths of threaded rod or studs end to end. The extended body engages many more threads than a standard nut, spreading load and resisting stripping in structural tie-rod and stud-coupling applications. Also available with NPT tapered threads where pipe joining is required.

Request Quote

Weld Nut

Hex and round nuts with projection bosses engineered for resistance spot welding onto sheet metal, creating a fixed female thread on panels too thin to tap. A staple of automotive body-in-white and appliance chassis fabrication.

Request Quote

Special Nut Per Drawing

Special nuts produced to your drawing — non-standard heights, thread sizes, slots, castellations, and combined nut-washer forms. Crown Link reviews the print, confirms thread gauging requirements, and sources from forging or machining lines as the geometry demands.

Request Quote

Custom Nut Per Drawing

Fully custom nut geometries — round bodies, internal hex sockets, knurled grips, integrated flanges, and press-in features. From a 3D model or 2D print, Crown Link quotes machined prototypes through high-volume cold-formed production.
