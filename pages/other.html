Other & Specialty

Sourcing rarely stops at screws and nuts. Crown Link's factory network extends to workholding clamps, springs, roller chain, wire rope assemblies, castings with secondary machining, threaded inserts, and complete kitted hardware sets. One accountable supplier, one quality system, one consolidated shipment — instead of a dozen vendor relationships to manage. Every item below carries the same 3-tier inspection discipline as our fastener lines.

Toggle Clamp

Workholding clamps using an over-center linkage that locks under load without sustained operator force, in horizontal, vertical, push-pull, and latch styles. Essential fixtures hardware for welding jigs, assembly stations, and inspection fixtures.

Cable Tension Adjuster

Inline barrel adjusters with knurled or hex bodies and fine-pitch threads that lengthen or shorten cable housing runs to set tension precisely. Found in automotive throttle and clutch cables, bicycle brake systems, and industrial control linkages.

Request Quote

Safety Cable

Stainless wire rope assemblies with swaged loop, stud, or ball terminals, proof-loaded and cut to length per your specification. Used as fall-arrest lanyards for signage and fixtures, hose whip restraints, and secondary retention on machinery guards.

Request Quote

Plastic & Rubber Assembly Parts

Non-metallic assembly hardware — EPDM and silicone grommets, nylon push-in clips and rivets, O-rings, bumpers, and wire management parts. Molded in Taiwan to standard catalogs or your print, in materials certified RoHS and REACH compliant.

Request Quote

Assembly Kit

Pre-kitted fastener sets — bolt, washers, nut, and related hardware counted, bagged, and labeled to your bill of materials. Kitting moves assembly-line picking errors and labor cost out of your plant and into Crown Link's packaging operation.

Request Quote

Roller Chain

ANSI B29.1 and ISO 606 roller chain in standard, heavy, and stainless series, supplied in cut lengths or reels with connecting and offset links. Power transmission hardware for conveyors, agricultural drives, and industrial machinery.

Request Quote

Springs

Compression, extension, and torsion springs wound from music wire, hard-drawn steel, and 302/316 stainless per your load and rate specification. Taiwan's spring industry supports prototype-to-production volumes with load testing documentation on request.

Request Quote

Large Casting & Machined Part

Castings in gray and ductile iron, aluminum, and zinc with secondary CNC machining of critical bores, faces, and threads. Crown Link manages pattern or die tooling, casting quality, and machining inspection as a single accountable supply chain.

Knurled Press-Fit Insert Bushing

Brass and steel inserts with diamond knurls that anchor by press-fit, heat-staking, or ultrasonic installation into plastic and composite parts. They convert molded bosses into durable machine threads for electronics housings and repeated-service assemblies.
