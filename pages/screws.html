Screws & Bolts

Crown Link sources screws and bolts across the full spectrum of Taiwan's forging capability — from M1.6 electronics hardware to M20+ structural bolting, in carbon, stainless, and alloy steels per ISO 898-1 and SAE J429. Every part below is available in standard catalog form or modified to your print, with thread gauging per ASME B1.1 and B1.13M verified before shipment. Specialty geometries — eccentric shoulders, break-away security heads, ball heads, extended lengths — are core capability, not exceptions.

Stainless Steel Screw

Hex head cap screws cold-forged in 304, 316, or 316L stainless steel with rolled threads per ASME B18.2.1 or DIN 933/931. Specified for food processing, marine, and outdoor structural applications where corrosion resistance is non-negotiable.

Open Die Screw

Screws formed on open tooling dies, enabling non-standard head profiles and low-to-mid volume runs without dedicated header tooling investment. The economical path to special head geometries when progressive die tooling cost cannot be justified.

Request Quote

SEMS Screw

Machine screws with washers captively assembled before thread rolling, so the washer can never be lost on the assembly line. Standard with flat, split lock, conical, or external tooth washers per IFI-500 series, widely used in appliance panels, electrical terminals, and PCB mounting.

Custom Screw Per Drawing

Fully custom screws sourced to your print — special head forms, shoulder diameters, thread combinations, drive recesses, and underhead features. Submit PDF, DWG, DXF, or STEP files and Crown Link matches the part to the qualified Gangshan factory with the right forming capability.

Small Size Screw

Precision machine screws in the M1.6–M4 range with pan, flat, and wafer heads and Phillips, slotted, or Torx drives per JIS B 1111 and ASME B18.6.3. The workhorse hardware of electronics enclosures, connectors, and small appliance assemblies.

Small Size Special Screw

Miniature screws with non-catalog features — specialty drives such as Torx and tri-wing, custom head forms, thread-forming points for plastics, and micro shoulder profiles. Built for electronics and precision device makers whose parts do not exist in any standard catalog.

Request Quote

Special Mid-Size Screw

Custom-formed screws in the M6–M10 range featuring shoulders, flanges, pilot points, and special thread engagement zones. The most common size class for automotive brackets, machinery sub-assemblies, and per-drawing OEM hardware.

Request Quote

Large Diameter Screw

Heavy hex bolts and cap screws from M20 upward — hot forged in Grade 8.8, 10.9, and 12.9 per ISO 898-1 or SAE Grade 5 and 8 per J429. Supplied with hot-dip galvanizing per ASTM A153 for structural, infrastructure, and heavy equipment bolting.

Request Quote

Ball Head Screw

Screws with a precision spherical head that mates with sockets and linkage cups to form articulating joints. Common in gas spring end fittings, throttle and shift linkages, and adjustable leveling assemblies.

Request Quote

Elevator Bolt

Flat countersunk-head bolts with a square neck that locks into the mating part to prevent rotation during nut tightening, per ASME B18.5. The standard fastener for bucket elevators, conveyor belting, and flooring where a flush, snag-free surface is required.

Request Quote

Eccentric Bolt

Bolts with an offset shoulder or cam lobe whose rotation translates into lateral position adjustment. The key component in automotive camber and alignment adjusters and machine positioning systems where controlled offset under torque is the design intent.

Request Quote

CAM Bolt

Two-part furniture cam-lock connectors — a dowel bolt that threads into one panel and a rotating cam disc set into the mating panel. A quarter turn of the cam draws the bolt head into its groove and clamps the joint. The standard tool-free fastener for ready-to-assemble furniture, cabinetry, and knock-down panel systems.

Request Quote

Triple Drive Screw

Screws with a combination recess accepting three driver types — typically Phillips, slotted, and Torx or square — in a single head. Specified for field-serviceable equipment where the end user's available tooling cannot be predicted.

Request Quote

Rubber Molded Screw

Screws over-molded with EPDM, NBR, or silicone collars that seal against panels and damp vibration at the joint. Used in HVAC housings, automotive trim, and electronics enclosures requiring an integrated seal without a separate washer.

Request Quote

Break-Away Head Security Screw

Tamper-resistant screws whose driving head shears off at a calibrated groove once installation torque is reached, leaving a smooth cone that cannot be gripped. Standard hardware for utility meters, public infrastructure, license plates, and theft-deterrent assemblies.

Request Quote

Long Special Screw

Extended-length screws with length-to-diameter ratios of 10:1 and beyond, produced with straightness control that ordinary forming lines cannot hold. Used in transformer cores, lighting fixtures, deep-boss plastic assemblies, and through-bolted machinery joints.
